Overview

Comprehensive After-Sales Support

Transformers are vital to the smooth running of any operation. That's why we have a comprehensive after-sales support service. Even if you have sourced your transformer(s) from another manufacturer, we can keep it in peak condition throughout its entire operational life.

What We Offer

Our Services

Installation & Acceptance Testing

On-site installation supervision and full commissioning tests to IEC and customer standards.

Onsite Repair

Rapid response field repairs minimizing downtime, with qualified engineers deployed to site.

Oil Purification

Mobile on-site oil processing units restoring insulating oil to original specification and extending transformer life.

Electrical Testing

Comprehensive diagnostic testing including insulation resistance, turns ratio, load loss, and no-load loss measurements.

Spare Parts

Genuine OEM spare parts available for all Elsewedy transformer models and most third-party units.

Maintenance Contracts

Flexible scheduled maintenance programs — annual, bi-annual, or custom intervals — to keep your fleet in peak condition.

Personnel Training

Certified training programs for operations and maintenance staff covering safety, diagnostics, and best practices.
