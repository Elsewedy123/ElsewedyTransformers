Overview

ESCART — Built for Safety

Elsewedy Electric CRT is the first of its kind in the Middle East and Africa. We produce transformers to suit any project, with power ratings up to 15 MVA and voltage up to 36 kV. Cast Resin Dry Type (ESCART) transformers are used by utility companies and T&D contractors in locations with densely-packed occupants, where safety is absolutely critical.
